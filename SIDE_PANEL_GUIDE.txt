WHAT IS A SIDE PANEL?
=====================

A side panel is a docked panel that opens on the right side of your browser
window. Unlike a popup that disappears when you click elsewhere, the side
panel stays open and visible while you browse.

HOW IT WORKS:
=============

1. Click the extension icon (paw print) in your toolbar
2. Side panel slides out from the right side
3. Shows full waitlist with all information
4. Panel stays open while you browse other tabs
5. Click extension icon again to close panel

RESIZING THE PANEL:
===================

Chrome/Edge allows you to resize the side panel:

1. Hover over the left edge of the panel
2. Cursor changes to resize cursor (â†”)
3. Click and drag left/right
4. Release to set width
5. Browser remembers your preferred width

======================

3. View waitlist in side panel

6. Side panel stays visible
7. Book appointment
8. Return to previous tab

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
===================

Chrome/Edge doesn't have default shortcuts for side panels, but you can:

1. Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts
2. Find "WKennel7 Waitlist Viewer"
3. Set a custom keyboard shortcut
4. Example: Ctrl+Shift+W

Then press your shortcut to toggle the side panel!
