## Services

Q: What services do you offer?
A: We offer bath only, full service grooming, and hand-stripping for certain terrier breeds.

Q: What's included in a bath?
A: Our bath service includes a double bath with premium shampoo, complete blow dry, thorough brush out, and nail trim.

Q: What's included in full service grooming?
A: Full service includes everything in our bath service plus a complete haircut styled to breed standard or your preference.

Q: What is hand-stripping?
A: Hand-stripping is a specialized grooming technique for wire-coated terrier breeds. It removes dead hair by hand rather than clipping. Only our groomer Kumi performs this service.

Q: Do you groom cats?
A: Yes, we groom cats as well as dogs.

Q: Do you do nail trims only?
A: Nail trims are included with our grooming services. For standalone nail services, please let me take your information and we'll have someone call you back.

Q: Do you express anal glands?
A: Yes, we can do that upon request. Just let us know when you drop off.

## Appointments

Q: How do I book an appointment?
A: I can take your information right now and have our team call you back to confirm, or you can book online at our website.

Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for Saturdays. We can sometimes fit in last-minute appointments depending on availability.

Q: Can I request a specific groomer?
A: Yes! Just let me know which groomer you prefer and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Q: How long does an appointment take?
A: Please allow up to 4 hours, though it's usually less. Time varies based on your pet's size and coat condition.

Q: Can I wait with my pet?
A: We don't have a waiting area, but we'll call you as soon as your pet is ready for pickup.

Q: Can I drop off early?
A: Your appointment time is your drop-off time. If you need to drop off earlier, please let us know when booking so we can try to accommodate.

Q: What if I'm running late?
A: Please call us as soon as you know you'll be late. We'll do our best to work with your schedule.
